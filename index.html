Contact 02 — Interview & CEA evaluation

Live interview used as the second filter in the CE Filtration System. Score the three CEA dimensions (max 6 pts) and capture qualitative answers. The final score determines the recommended initial Tier.

CEA Entry Score · 3 dimensions

Rate each dimension based on how the applicant responds during the call. Good = 2 · Medium = 1 · Poor = 0.

Communication · Does the applicant answer clearly, specifically, and in an understandable way?
Availability · Defined schedules? Solution-oriented attitude?
Declared quality · Real experience, handling of difficult situations

Qualitative questions

Short answers captured during the call. These feed the Declared quality dimension above.

How do you handle a job that doesn’t go as planned?
Describe a real difficult situation you have handled on the job.
References we can contact
How flexible are you with last-minute changes or urgent jobs?
Do you prefer larger jobs or several smaller ones during the week?
Additional notes from the call (tone, attitude, red flags, etc.)

Validate job · CEP score

Post-job evaluation (manual interno § 06). Score the partner on the three CEP dimensions. Max 6 pts. This score is averaged with CEA to recalculate the partner's Tier.

CEP Performance Score · 3 dimensions

Good = 2 · Medium = 1 · Poor = 0.

Execution · Was the job done correctly and completely?
Communication · Did the partner respond on time? Any communication issues?
Reliability · Did they arrive on time? Deliver what was promised?
